Privacy Policy – Rishta Biodata

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Rishta Biodata ("the App") is a marriage biodata maker that helps you create and share biodata PDFs. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Data Collection

Rishta Biodata does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter — including name, family details, photos, and contact information — is stored exclusively on your device and used only to generate your biodata PDF.

2. No Internet Access

The App works completely offline. It does not communicate with any external servers, APIs, or third-party services. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share the generated PDF.

3. Photos

Photos you add to your biodata are stored temporarily on your device solely for the purpose of generating the PDF. They are not uploaded or shared anywhere without your explicit action.

4. No Advertising

The App does not display advertisements and does not use any advertising SDKs or tracking identifiers.

5. No Analytics

The App does not use any analytics tools, crash reporting services, or usage tracking of any kind.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
