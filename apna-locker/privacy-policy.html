Privacy Policy – Apna Locker

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Apna Locker ("the App") is a personal credential and password manager designed to store your data securely on your device. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Data Collection

Apna Locker does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter — including passwords, usernames, and notes — is stored exclusively on your device.

2. No Internet Access

The App does not require an internet connection and does not communicate with any external servers, APIs, or third-party services. Your data never leaves your device.

3. Local Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using encrypted local storage. We do not have access to this data at any time.

4. No Advertising

The App does not display advertisements and does not use any advertising SDKs or tracking identifiers.

5. No Analytics

The App does not use any analytics tools, crash reporting services, or usage tracking of any kind.

6. Permissions

The App may request biometric authentication (fingerprint/face unlock) permission solely to protect access to your stored credentials. This is handled entirely by your device's operating system and is never accessible to us.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
